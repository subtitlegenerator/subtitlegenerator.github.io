How to convert WebM to MP4?
Browsers can only export WebM video. To convert to MP4:
  • Use HandBrake (free, Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Or VLC (free, Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Or an online converter
  • Or with ffmpeg:
    ffmpeg -i input.webm -c:v libx264 -crf 23 -preset veryfast output.mp4

What is Subtitle Generator?

Subtitle Generator is a free online tool that lets you create professional subtitle videos with green screen, blue screen, or black backgrounds—directly in your browser. No downloads, no watermarks, and no sign-up required. Perfect for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels, and any video editor that supports chroma keying. All processing is done locally for maximum privacy and speed.

How does it work? (Step-by-step guide)

  1. Enter your script in the text box, or upload an SRT file with your subtitles and timings.
  2. Choose your font, subtitle color, and background color (green, blue, or black screen).
  3. (Optional) Enable animated text effects or keyword highlighting for extra impact.
  4. Click Generate Subtitles to instantly preview your video.
  5. Download your subtitle video (WebM, perfect for editing) or export the SRT file for use elsewhere.
