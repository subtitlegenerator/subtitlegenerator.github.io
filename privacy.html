Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information. Any data you provide (such as subtitle text or files) is processed in your browser and not stored on our servers.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to generate subtitles and is not shared or retained after you leave the site.

3. Cookies

This site does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party advertising services. External links may have their own privacy policies.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how visitors use the site and improve our service. Google Analytics collects information such as your browser type, device, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here. If you wish to opt out, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Data Security

All subtitle processing is done locally in your browser. Analytics data is processed by Google and not shared with other parties.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the site means you accept the revised policy.
